




                                          Salt and Pepper Walnuts


             10 cups of walnuts 2 lbs.
             10 Tbs butter melted
             5 tsp pepper
             4 tsp sea salt ( one tsp of which can be smoked sea salt). 
             


         Bake for 30 min. at 300 degrees.
         Experiment with other spices and flavors as you wish.
