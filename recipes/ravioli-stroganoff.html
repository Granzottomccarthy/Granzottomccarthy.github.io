Ravioli Stroganoff

From the kitchen of: Kim Granzotto

INGREDIENTS:


INSTRUCTIONS:

Fry pearl onions in oil, when almost done add the garlic and mushrooms or beef, fry until tender.

Add the gravy, cream cheese, mustard, salt and pepper to onions. Cook until warmed through.

Cook the ravioli until done in boiling water.

Pour gravy mix over the ravioli.

Serve with parmesian cheese.
