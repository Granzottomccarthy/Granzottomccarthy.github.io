Herb Brown Rice

From the kitchen of: Peg Granzotto

INGREDIENTS:


INSTRUCTIONS:

Add rosemary, basil, and bay leaf to rice.

Cook rice as on label.

Cook celery, mushrooms and garlic in butter until celery is tender but not brown.

Sprinkle top with parsley.

Just brown in oven but do not bake.

By adding the herbs to the rice before cooking add alot of flavor.


This recipe was from the Zion Lutheran Church cookbook and submitted by Arlene Sage.
