INSTRUCTIONS:

In medium bowl, mix the first 8 items.

Blend together honey (slightly warmed in microwave ), water and almond extract.

NOTE: I find best results when honey, water, almond extract, are measured accurately.

It may sometimes be necessary to add a drop or two of water if too dry, but too moist of a mixture is not desired. Dry is better than wet dough.

Add this mixture to dry ingredients and mix until well blended.

On a lightly floured surface, divide dough in half.

Form each portion into a log about 12" by 2" and 3/4" thick*

If longer biscotti are desired make one large log 12" by 4" and 3/4" thick

Place on a baking sheet with parchment paper.

Bake 30 minutes in a 350 degree oven.Remove and cool for 10 minutes.

Place log on a cutting board and with a serrated knife cut log diagonally into 3/4 inch slices using a sawing motion

Place slices back on the baking sheet, cut side up, and bake 15 more minutes.

Remove from oven and place the biscotti on a wire rack to cool.
