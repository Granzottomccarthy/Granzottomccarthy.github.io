INSTRUCTIONS:

Set oven temperature to 350 degrees.

Sift together white flour, soda, salt, and sugar.

Add graham flour and cut in butter

Add cracked wheat, egg, and buttermilk to make a soft dough.

Mix only long enough to form a mass. Do not kneed.

If too dry, add small amount of buttermilk.

Form into a round loaf and place in a greased 9 inch cake pan.

Dust top with flour and make a cross on top.

Bake 50 minutes.

Variations......................

Add a handful of raisins

Substitute rolled oats for cracked wheat.
