Alfredo Sauce

From the kitchen of: Joel Kovach

INGREDIENTS:


INSTRUCTIONS:

Melt butter in medium sauce pan over medium heat.

Stir in flour until will blended.

Gradually stir in half and half, water and bouillon cubes.

Cook, stirring constantly until sauce is slightly thickened.

Add mushrooms, stir in parmesan cheese and heat until melted.

Serve over pasta.


Options:

Add smoked shrimp or chicken

1/2 cup white wine
