INSTRUCTIONS:

Cream butter and sugars.

Beat in egg yolks.

Add flour and rolled oats and mix well.

Spread in 13x9 inch pan.

Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.

Cool 10 minutes.

Melt chocolate with butter.

Spread over cooled cookie layer.

Sprinkle with nuts.


P.S. I usually melted the chocolate directly on the cookies when I took them out of the oven.

My recipe does not give the amount of butter to use with the chocolate.
