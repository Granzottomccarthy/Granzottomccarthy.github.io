Cheese Broccoli Soup

From the kitchen of: Kim Granzotto

Prep: 1 hour

Cook: __ mins

Total:__ mins

Number of servings: 8


INGREDIENTS:


INSTRUCTIONS:

Melt butter in fry pan, add onion, carrots, potatoes, celery, broccoli, green pepper and sauté until tender.

Add broth.

Slowly add milk to flour,then combine with broth and veggies in large pot.

Bring to a boil and thicken for 5 to 10 min.

Add cheese whiz, salt, and pepper to taste.

Reduce heat and simmer for another 10 min.

Be creative and add any other veggies or diced chicken.

Serve with homemade bread.
