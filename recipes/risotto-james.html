Risotto (James’s)

From the kitchen of: James Granzotto

Number of servings: 3 people


INGREDIENTS:


INSTRUCTIONS:

In a sauce pan add the olive oil and sauté the onion until transparent.

Add the garlic and cook for only one minute. Immediately add the can of tomato sauce and stir into the onion/garlic mixture.

Add salt and pepper to taste and cook for one hour to blend all the ingredients.

Add wine the last 5 min. of cooking.

In a 4 quart pot add the sauce mixture at medium heat. Add the rice and toast the rice for a few minutes but do not burn.

Ladle in a cup of simmering stock, stirring the rice constantly. When the liquid has almost been absorbed by the rice, add another 1/2 cup of hot stock.

Continue stirring and adding the remaining stock 1/2 cup at a time for 30 minutes to cook the arborio rice.

The rice should be tender but a bit firm. Testing is what cooks do, so test often.
