Soft Pretzels

From the kitchen of: John Granzotto

Prep:30 mins

Cook: 20 mins

Number of servings: 20

INSTRUCTIONS:

In a large mixing bowl, mix 2 Tbs. warm water, with the yeast to dissolve it.

Let rise for 10 min. Then stir in the remaining warm water and brown sugar.

Place bowl on mixer with a dough hook, gradually add the flour and continue mixing until it forms a ball.

Turn it out onto a work surface and knead the dough to make it smooth.

Use extra flour on work surface. Let rise for 1 hour.

Preheat oven to 475 degrees.

Butter 2 sheet pans. Bring 2 quarts of water and the baking soda to boil in a large saucepan.

Cut dough up into golf ball size pieces.

Roll them into 1/2 inch thick,15 in. long worms.

Form a U then cross the ends, twisting at the middle.

Fold the ends back down to meet the U (which is now an O) and press to secure the dough.

Drop it into the boiling water for 30 seconds then fish it out and place it on a greased sheet pan.

Sprinkle with kosher salt.

Repeat with the rest of the dough.

Bake for 8 minutes or until golden brown.
