INSTRUCTIONS:

Cut any beef or turkey, venison, into 1/2 inch thick pieces. Works best when half frozen.

Marinate for at least 12 hours or longer in refrigerator. Take from brine and let drain on cooking racks, can be crowded on racks. Put meat on smoker with wood chips or in 158 degree oven until desired doneness.

Can take up to 12 hours to cook depending on the temperature.!!
