Pizza Bread Dough

From the kitchen of: John Granzotto

Number of servings: makes 2 pizza's


INGREDIENTS:


INSTRUCTIONS:

1/2 tsp sugar, dissolved in the hot water

1 pkg. Quick rise yeast, sprinkled in hot water, allow to foam, about 10 min

Add 1 cup cold water, mix slowly in;

3 1/2 cups flour and 2 tsp salt in a large bowl

Kneed by hand or machine until all flour makes a ball.

Let rest for 5 min. covered

Kneed 50 strokes or mix for 5 min

Let rest for 2 min, covered

Kneed 20 strokes or 2 min in mixer

Let rise in same bowl, with a coat of oil

Cover and keep in warm spot for 30 min until double in size

Pat dough down in rectangle and fold in 3, repeat

Let rise for 45 min, about triple in size

Shape to desired pizza shape on plastic sheet.

Cover and let rest for 10 min

Place upside down on baking pan and peel off

Let rest 5 min. and add toppings

Bake 425 to 450 for 18 to 20 min , turning half way through until brown, 15 min on a stone.
