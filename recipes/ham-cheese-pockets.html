Ham & Cheese Pockets

From the kitchen of: Kim Granzotto


INGREDIENTS:


INSTRUCTIONS:

Warm a flour tortilla wrap, put sliced ham in center of wrap.

Add half of the cheese, make a well and crack the egg in the middle.

Put more cheese on top of egg.

Add salt and pepper, and any other topping you like (Franks, pepper flakes, chives, spinach).

Spray a square of alluminum foil with butter spray.

Put tortilla on foil and wrap tortilla up, seam side down.

Bake in 350 degree oven for 25 minutes.
