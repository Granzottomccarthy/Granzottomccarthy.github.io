INSTRUCTIONS:

Boil eggs for 35 seconds and cool

Add eggs to mixture by breaking eggs and spooning out

Clean lettuce and tear by twisting off top and bottom halfs

Roll lemons in hand, then squeeze into bowl with strainer for seeds

Pour or shred parmesan cheese

Add black pepper and croutons

Mix well and serve
