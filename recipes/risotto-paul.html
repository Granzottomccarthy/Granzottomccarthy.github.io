Risotto Paul's (without tomato sauce)

From the kitchen of: Paul Granzotto

Prep:__ mins

Cook: As Long As it Takes

Total:__ mins

Number of servings: 6


INGREDIENTS:


INSTRUCTIONS:

In a 4 quart sauce pan, bring to boil 8 cups of water and add bullion.

Reduce heat to a gentle simmer and add wine and saffron.

In a large sauce pan, saute onion in olive oil until soft and transparent.

Add the crushed garlic and cook for one min. longer.

Add rice and cook over med. heat for a few minutes, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon allowing the rice to toast a little.

*Pour yourself a glass of the Merlot. Sip.

Add 1 cup of the broth to the rice mixture, stirring constantly until the rice absorbs the broth.

Turn the heat down to low and add 1/2 cup (2 full ladles) of stock and continue to cook stirring constantly.

Continue to stir and add more stock as it is absorbed and until rice is tender but firm, about minutes.

Add salt and pepper to taste. Pour your second glass of wine. Sip.

Stir in grated parmesan cheese and chopped parsley.

Serve immediately with a full glass of wine.

This special Risotto recipe was passed on to us by Nona (Giavona)Granzotto. It is a wonderful reminder of her, her parents before her, and our Italian heritage who shared this meal countless times with their families. Risotto has been a main staple in the Granzotto family for many generations and similar to the careful time it takes to prepare this meal, we are reminded of its likeness to giving the quality time and tender care to ourselves and our loved ones. Take the time to fold together these simple ingredients. Not too quickly! Don’t wait too long! Spend the right amount of time to prepare this wonderful meal so that it can be enjoyed. Feel free to add additional ingredients and spices as needed to meet your family’s taste.
