INSTRUCTIONS:

Mix flour with cinnamon, salt, soda, baking powder.

Add a small amount to mixture.

Fold in nuts and carrots.

Bake in a 9 in cake pan lined with wax paper, for 50 min at 350 degrees for tin and 325 for glass pan.

This recipe is loved by all, but is especially made for Dad for his Birthday.

You can add some cream cheese frosting or cool whip over top. Add some strawberries too.
