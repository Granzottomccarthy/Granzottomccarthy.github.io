INSTRUCTIONS:

Melt butter and cocoa in pan. Allow to cool.

Add sugar and eggs, vanilla and mix well.

Add flour and salt to mixture.

Pour into a 9 by 13 pan

Bake for 35 to 45 min, in a 350 degree oven.

While cake is hot add marshmallows to top of cake.

Put back into oven for a few minutes until marshmallows are melted and puffy.

Spread icing on hot cake
