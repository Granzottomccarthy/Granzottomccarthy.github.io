Cinnamon Rolls - Old Fashioned

From the kitchen of: Anna Kegel

Number of servings: 24


INGREDIENTS:


INSTRUCTIONS:

In large bowl, combine 1/2 cup sugar, yeast, salt and 2-1/4 cups flour.

Heat margarine and water to lukewarm.

Add to flour mixture and beat well until smooth.

Add eggs, beat well.

Add 3/4 cup flour and beat well.

Add remaining 3-3/12 cups flour to form a stiff dough.

Turn out on floured surface.

Kneed 5 minutes and form a large ball.

Place dough in large greased bowl.

Turn dough to grease top.

Cover with towel and place in warm spot to rise.

Allow to double about 2 hours.

Punch down and divide in half.

Roll out half in rectangle, 12x18 inches.

Sprinkle with half the cinnamon and brown sugar, raisins, and nuts.

Roll up and cut into 12 slices

Place in pan 9x13 inches that is well greased.

Repeat with other half.

Cover and let rise until double, about 1 1/2 hours.

Bake at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes until brown.

Frost with confectioners sugar glaze.
