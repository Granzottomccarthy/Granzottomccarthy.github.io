Chicken Wraps

From the kitchen of: John Granzotto

Number of servings: 3


INGREDIENTS:


INSTRUCTIONS:

Cut the breast into 4 slices lengthwise and wrap a piece of meat around it.

Bake in 350 degree oven until chicken is done and meat crisp. About 20 min.


We were up north at a cabin with no spices and lots of great Italian meat (thanks Mom and Dad) and a package of chicken. This is the meal John came up with and it was very good.

It would also make a great appetizer.
