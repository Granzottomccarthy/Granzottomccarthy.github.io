INSTRUCTIONS:

Heat olive oil in a medium sauce pan and over medium heat Add the garlic and cook for only one minute stirring constantly. Do not burn the garlic as it will become bitter.

Add the basil, tomatoes, salt and pepper.

When the mixture comes to a boil, reduce the heat to low and allow to simmer for 20 min. uncovered, stirring occasionally. Cooking as much as one hour will give a thicker sauce.

Makes a generous 2 cups of sauce, enough for a pound of pasta for 4 persons. This can be used for any preparation calling for tomato sauce.
