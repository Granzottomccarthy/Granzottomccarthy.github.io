INSTRUCTIONS:

Divide Dough per serving size.

Roll dough into an 8 inch circle.

Spread sauce onto of dough leaving approx. 2 inches from the edge.

Add remaining toppings.

Fold dough in half. Seal edges together.

Cut 2 slits on top.

Place on a cooking sheet dusted with cornmeal.

Bake at 350 for 20 minutes or until lightly browned.

Remove from oven.

Brush with olive oil, sprinkle with parmesan cheese and Italian seasonings.

Serve.
