INSTRUCTIONS:

Spray the inside of a bag with pam. Put corn in bag.

In a large bowl mix brown sugar, oleo, microwave on high to rapid boil once.

Add soda, stir 1 minute or until foamy, pour over pop corn in bag.

Roll bag closed.

Shake to coat.

Prick bag with pin and microwave on high 1 1/2 minutes.

Take out and shake.

Microwave 1 1/2 minutes and shake again.

Microwave 1/2 minute twice and shake twice.

Pour out onto a cookie sheet to cool and air dry.
