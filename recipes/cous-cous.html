Cous Cous

From the kitchen of: James Granzotto

INGREDIENTS:


INSTRUCTIONS:

Rinse and drain cous cous. Place in a 2 qt. bowl.

Squeeze 2 lemons over cous cous and let soak.

Chop the tomato, cucumber parsley, and mint leaves very fine and place on top of cous cous.

Chop onion very fine.

Sprinkle black pepper on onion, mix pepper into onion by hand (reduces sharpness of the onion).

Put onion in cous cous and mix well.

5 minutes before serving add the olive oil and salt, mix well.
