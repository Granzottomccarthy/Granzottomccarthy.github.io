Chicken Stock

From the kitchen of: James Granzotto

INGREDIENTS:


INSTRUCTIONS:

Place the chicken in a 12-quart soup pot and rinse with very hot tap water.

Drain and add the cold tap water.

Bring to a simmer, skimming off any foam.

Add the other ingredients, bring to a boil and cook for 2 hours on low for a slow boil.

The stock will taste a bit flat since it has no salt. Salt will be added when you use the stock in preparation of soups, sauces, pasta, risotto or stews.

After the chicken has cooled a little, it can be deboned and used for chicken cacciatore.

Topped with Simple Tomato Sauce this makes a great dish.
