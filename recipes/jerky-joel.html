INSTRUCTIONS:

Use extra lean ground meat.

Mix seasoning and cure together according to chart above.

Add 1/2 c. ice water per lb. of meat.

Mix thoroughly for approx. 5 minutes or until sticky

Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or over night.

I use a jerky press but it inn’ necessary.

If you don’t have a press.

Start with a flat board or cookie sheet, etc. and wax paper.

lay seasoned meat between 2 large sheets of wax paper. Roll meat with a rolling pin into a large rectangle. Approx. 1/4” thick .

Cut into 1” wide strips.

Remove top layer of wax paper .

Place a will buttered jerky screen on strips.

Place one hand under the board and one on top of screen and flip over so that jerky is on top of the screen.

Remove board and wax paper.

Place screen with jerky in 200 degree oven on top rack for 1 hour for 20 minutes
