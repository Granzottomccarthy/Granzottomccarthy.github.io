Cinnamon-Roasted Almonds

From the kitchen of: James Granzotto

Prep: 15 mins

Cook: 1 hour


INGREDIENTS:


INSTRUCTIONS:

Preheat oven to 250 degrees.

Lightly grease a 10 x 15 inch jellyroll pan, or any pan, not to overlap nuts.<

Lightly beat the egg white; add water, and beat until frothy but not stiff.

Add the nuts, and stir until well coated. Mix the sugar, salt, and cinnamon, and sprinkle over the nuts. Toss to coat, and spread evenly on the prepared pan.

Bake one hour in the preheated oven, stirring occasionally, until golden.

Allow to cool, then store nuts in airtight containers.


Here is an easy snack idea to serve at any holiday party.
