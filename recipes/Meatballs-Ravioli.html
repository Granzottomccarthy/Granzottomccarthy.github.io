Meatballs and Ravioli Bake

From the kitchen of: Kim Granzotto

INGREDIENTS:


INSTRUCTIONS:

Put one cup of marinara sauce in bottom of casserole dish 9x13

Layer the cooked meatballs over sauce.

Layer the cooked cheese ravioli over the meatballs.

Cover with remaining marinara.

Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over top.

Bake 350 degrees for 45 min or until bubbly.

Tips; Soak one flour tortilla or pita bread in milk, add the ground beef and pork, 2 Tbsp. Italian seasoning, 1/4 cup of parmesan cheese, 2 eggs, salt and pepper. Mix meatballs and scoop and roll into balls. Put on baking sheet and cook meatballs for 45 min (depending how big they are. I make mine close to the same size as the ravioli).
