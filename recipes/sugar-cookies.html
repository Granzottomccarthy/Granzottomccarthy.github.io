Decorated Sugar Cookies

From the kitchen of: Joyce McCarthy

Cook: 8 mins

Number of servings: 30 cookies


INGREDIENTS:


INSTRUCTIONS:

Add sifted dry ingredients to creamed mixture.

Shape into mound, wrap in waxed paper and chill thoroughly.

Roll on board lightly “floured” with powdered sugar, until dough is 1/4 inch thick.

Dip cutter in powdered sugar each time before cutting cookie, then place cookie on lightly greased baking sheet.

Bake in moderate (375 degree) oven for about 8 min., or until delicately browned.

FROSTING:

Decorate with any color you wish.

Never use oil base coloring.


Note:This is Joyce McCarthy's famous sugar cookie recipe. She made around 900 cookies at Christmas, and about half that on the other holidays. She donated some to the Ronald McDonald house and also gave alot away as gifts.
