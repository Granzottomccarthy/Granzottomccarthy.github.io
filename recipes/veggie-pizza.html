Veggie Pizza

From the kitchen of: Joyce McCarthy

Prep:__

Cook:__

Total:__

Number of servings:__


INGREDIENTS:

Can use any vegies you want. You can also top with parmesan cheese.


INSTRUCTIONS:

Heat oven to 375 degrees

unroll and press onto ungreased baking sheet. press to make sides,(15x10x1)

Bake 13 to 17 min or until golden brown, about 30 min

In small bowl mix cream cheese, sour cream, dill and garlic powder until smooth. Spread over crust. Top with veggies.

Serve immediately or cover and refrigerate 1 to 2 hours .

Cut into 8 rows by 4 rows.

This is a great appetizer for get togethers and showers.
