Mix together everything but potatoes and parmesan cheese.

Break up frozen potatoes and add to mixture.

Place in greased 9 X 13 pan.

Dot with butter.

Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.

Refrigerate for 6 hours or overnight.

Bake at 35 Deg for 1 hour until golden and bubbly.
