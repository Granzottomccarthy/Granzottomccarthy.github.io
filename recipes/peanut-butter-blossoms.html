INSTRUCTIONS:

Heat oven to 375 degrees

Stir in flour, baking soda, baking powder until dough formes.

In large bowl beat 1/2 cup sugar, brown sugar,peanut butter and egg , mix until well blended.

Refrigerate for 30 min. or more to prevent the cookie from spreading out.

Shape dough into 1 inch balls, 1 1/2 oz scoop.

Roll into the aditional sugar.

Place on ungreased cookie sheet 2 inches apart.

Bake 8 to 10 min. or until edges are light brown.

Immediatly press one chocolate in center of cookie.

Remove from pan and put on cooling rack.
