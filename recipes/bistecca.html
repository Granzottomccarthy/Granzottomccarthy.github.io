Bistecca Pizzaiola

From the kitchen of: Peg Granzotto

Cook: 20 mins

Number of servings: 3-4


INGREDIENTS:


INSTRUCTIONS:

Heat oil and butter in skillet over medium-high heat.

Add meat and brown on all sides.

Transfer to platter.

Add mushrooms to skillet and saute 3 to 5 min.

Remove with slotted spoon and place over meat.

Add onion and garlic, saute 3 to 5 min.

Add tomatoes and mash slightly with fork.

Stir in parsley and oregano.

Season with salt and pepper.

Simmer 30 min.

Stir in wine and cheese-simmer 15 min.

Return meat and mushrooms to skillet.

Cover and simmer 1 hour. Stir occasionally.
