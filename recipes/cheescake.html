From the kitchen of: Joyce McCarthy

Topping;


INSTRUCTIONS:

Prepare the graham cracker crust. Put into pie pan.

Whip the eggs.

Add and mix well the cream cheese , vanilla and sugar.

Add the eggs. Beat until smooth and creamy.

Bake for 35 minutes at 350 degrees.

Cool.

Put strawberries and cornstarch in a sauce pan and bring to a boil.

Cool then pour over cheesecake.

This is recipe was always requested for Christmas but has been requested for other occasions as well.
