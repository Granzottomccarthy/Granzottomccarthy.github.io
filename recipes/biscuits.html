INSTRUCTIONS:

In a 12 inch cast-iron pan add 1 tablespoon of shortening.

Place pan in the oven and set the temperature at 500 degrees

Mix flour, soda, baking powder and salt.

Cut in the 2 tablespoons of shortening.

Stir in the buttermilk and kneed the mixture enough to make the dough into a ball that holds together.

Pat out onto a flowered table to 1/2 inch thickness.

Cut the dough in 2 inch circles and place in the hot cast-iron pan.

Turn each biscuit once and place back in the oven for 10 minutes.
